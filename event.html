    About The Run

    The Pride of Dharwad is the ideal venue for bringing together runners and all citizens to celebrate their running journey. Over the years, running in Bharat has increased exponentially, and Dharwad is one of the cities part of this transformation. The running community in Dharwad is budding and is all bound to grow more in the coming years. Dharwad is all set for its first edition of Dharwad Half Marathon, which will pass by significant local landmarks. We think this event will significantly increase the visibility of running in the city and encourage a fit and healthy lifestyle.

    Rules

  • Registrants will receive an email and SMS with a special code after their application has been submitted successfully.
  • To submit your entries, only use the suggested application methods on this page.
  • Before submitting your application, please carefully review all pertinent information provided on the event website and application form.
  • Requests to cancel participation and receive a refund won't be taken into consideration.
  • If attendees are found to be rude or abusive toward the event's organisers, volunteers, vendors, or third-party contractors, the organisers reserve the right to prohibit them from future events.
  • Please be aware that you won't be able to obtain an official finish time and, consequently, the official timing certificate reflecting your finish times, if you tamper in any way with the bib tag or remove/exchange it with another runner. With no chip, no timing, and no prizes.
  • Only the racing category for which you applied and whose entry has been confirmed are the ones in which you are allowed to compete. Your entry, running number bib, and/or bib tag cannot, under any circumstances, be transferred to another individual.
  • ONLY APPLICABLE IN CASE OF THE NEW WAVE OF COVID-19 or ANY OTHER EXTERNAL DISTURBANCE LEADING TO FORCE MAJEURE - There will be no future adjustment / carry forward of the fees towards any other event in any case, and the race format will be changed into a NON - TIMED Virtual Race for everyone. The physical takeaways like Race T-SHIRT, Finisher Medal (based on finish status) & other items (if any) shall be sent via courier 50 days after the virtual event.
  • The Promoter maintains the right to disqualify or exclude from the event/competition any person who provides false information about themselves on the entry form, who has been barred from competing by the IAAF, or who is suspected of using drugs that are prohibited. There will be no admission fee refund.
  • Runners who do not begin their run at the appropriate start timings for the race categories they have been confirmed in will not receive a result or certificate.
  • If, in the view of the medical staff, a participant is deemed to be medically unsuitable to continue, the organisers reserve the right to prevent them from participating.
  • Participants are urged to work with the police and security officers stationed at the event locations and along the route. On race day, there may be random checks of participants and supporters. This is for your security.
  • Photographs of runners taken during the pre-race event and on race day may be used to advertise the event.
