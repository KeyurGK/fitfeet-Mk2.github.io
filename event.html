    About The Run

    The Pride of Dharwad is the ideal venue for bringing together runners from all across Karnataka to celebrate their running journey. Over the years, running in Bharat has increased exponentially, and Dharwad is one of the cities part of this transformation. The running community in Dharwad is budding and is all bound to grow more in the coming years. Dharwad is all set for its first edition of annual city run, which will pass by significant local landmarks. We think this event will significantly increase the visibility of running in the city and encourage a fit and healthy lifestyle.
